  1. OV-Chipcard/Ov-ChipKaart

    What is an ov-chipkaart?
    The OV-chipkaart is a smart card used for public transportation in the Netherlands. It's a reusable, reloadable card that you can use to pay for train, bus, tram, and metro journeys instead of buying tickets each time you travel.

    To get an OV-Chipkaart at Schiphol airport:

    1. Visit the OV-chipkaart counter near the main entrance of Schiphol Plaza
    2. Choose between a personal or anonymous OV-chipkaart
    3. Provide ID for personal card
    4. Load card with credit
    5. Use card by checking in/out of public transportation.
